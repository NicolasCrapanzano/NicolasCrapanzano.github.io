Markus persson Aka "Notch"

Creator of minecraft

Relevant information

  • 1979 - Born on Stockholm, Sweden.
  • 1986 - Started programming with a Commodore 128 computer at age 7.
  • 2004 - At age 18 he started working with Midasplayer, later known as King.com. Where he met a developer called Jakob Porsér and became friends.
  • March 2009 - Persson and Porsér started working on their own games some for events like game jams. This caused them some problems with their employers, Midasplayer, wich prompted Persson to leave the company and join jAlbum.
  • May 2009 - Notch spent one week building a prototype for a 3D cave simulator, based on another game called "Infiniminer" by Zachtronics. The game consisted on a Lego-like playstyle, where everything was made out of cubes, wich you could remove and add. The first release of the game was made privately on the TigIRC forums the 16 of may, and a public version known as classic released was the next day and started seeing some small succes among the users of the forum.
